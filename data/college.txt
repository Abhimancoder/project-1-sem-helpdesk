Shri Ramswaroop Memorial University (SRMU), Lucknow

General Information:
SRMU is a private university located in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and doctoral programs across multiple disciplines.

Courses Offered:
1. Engineering (B.Tech, M.Tech)
2. Computer Applications (BCA, MCA)
3. Management (BBA, MBA)
4. Pharmacy (B.Pharma, D.Pharma)
5. Law (LLB, BA LLB)
6. Science (B.Sc, M.Sc)
7. Commerce (B.Com)
8. Arts and Humanities (BA, MA)
9. Polytechnic Diploma Courses

Admission Process:
1. Students must have passed 10+2 (for UG courses) with minimum 45–50% marks.
2. For PG courses, a relevant graduation degree is required.
3. Admission is based on merit or entrance exam.
4. Students can apply online through the university website or visit campus.

Documents Required:
1. 10th and 12th Marksheet
2. Transfer Certificate (TC)
3. Character Certificate
4. Passport size photographs
5. Aadhaar Card or ID Proof
